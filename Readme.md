Land Of New Towns

Author:   	AnT
Mod homepage:	https://sites.google.com/view/landofnewtowns

Mod contains:
+ 15 new towns
+ 3rd upgrades
+ 230 new creatures
+ 30 new map objects
+ 9 new artifacts

This mod adds 15 new towns and creatures to the game:  Wonderwoods, Eldorado, SandTown, ShadowTown, Neutral Town, ForestTown, Bastion(Pavilion), Druids town, Cove, Knights Castle, Mythology, Techno town. Total number of towns is 24 with no replacements.

------------------------------------------------
Installation:
------------------------------------------------
Base Platform: HoMM III + ERA WoG 3.9.x (Enabled via Mod Manager).
Required Mods: WoG (Core), WoG/ERA Scripts, GEM, ERA ERM Framework.
Supported Add-ons: XXL-mod, Quick Savings, Spell Descriptions, Battle Speed, Battle Queue, Human AI, Hero Reserve Slots.
Note: Disable all other mods to avoid conflicts.

Important! 
* Add <Fix.Cosmetic> = 0 to the Tweaks tab in the HoMM3 HD launcher. 
  This is required for the Bastion town screen to display correctly. 

* Disable "Week of monsters" in WoGify Options (under the "Map Enhancements and Artifacts" tab).

Incompatible mods:
"TUM Enchanted", "ResOunD", "ERA+", "Towns of Day and Night", "Knightmare Kingdoms", "H3-HD-Edition", "TUM-Enchanted-Graphic-Pack", "JS - new objects".
To resolve conflicts, select the Land of New Towns mod in the Mod Manager and click the Sort button; all incompatible mods will be disabled automatically.
